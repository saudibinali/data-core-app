CANONICAL FINANCE ARCHITECTURE SUMMARY
--------------------------------------
Ten model groups (design only):
  finance_fiscal_years, finance_periods, finance_chart_of_accounts,
  finance_journals, finance_journal_entries, finance_posting_batches,
  finance_cost_centers, finance_dimensions (+ values),
  finance_account_mappings, finance_locks

Principles:
  - Workspace isolation on all entities
  - Double-entry with immutable posted lines
  - Prepare vs post separation (post deferred)
  - Idempotent posting batches from payroll runs
  - Distinct payroll periods vs finance periods

CHART OF ACCOUNTS STRATEGY
--------------------------
  - Hierarchical COA with postable leaf accounts
  - Account types: asset, liability, equity, revenue, expense
  - Workspace templates (generic SME, KSA payroll, IFRS-lite)
  - Bilingual names (name + name_ar)
  - Validation against free-text payroll component codes

JOURNAL / LEDGER STRATEGY
-------------------------
  - Journal headers (PAYROLL, GEN, ADJ, REV)
  - Posting batches group entries with idempotency keys
  - Lifecycle: prepared → reviewed → posted (post not implemented)
  - Reversals via mirror batches; corrections via delta batches
  - P21-D GlJournalLine maps to prepared entries

PAYROLL FINANCE STRATEGY
------------------------
  - Locked final/correction runs gate finance prepare
  - Mapping: finance_account_mappings > component codes > legacy gl_account_code
  - Cost center: component override > finance_cost_centers > org unit
  - FinancialExportService remains export-prep layer until P22-B persists batches
  - Correction runs produce delta batches vs original

FINANCE PERIOD & LOCK STRATEGY
------------------------------
  - Fiscal years + monthly finance periods (open / soft_close / hard_close)
  - finance_locks independent of payroll_locks
  - Break-glass with audit; payroll/finance lock coordination policies

COST CENTER & DIMENSIONS
------------------------
  - finance_cost_centers master replaces org-unit name fallback
  - Dimensions: DEPARTMENT, BRANCH, PROJECT
  - Direct allocation default; split rules future

FINANCIAL REPORTING STRATEGY
----------------------------
  - finance.* report namespace (trial balance, journal detail — future)
  - Continued hr.payroll.* operational exports
  - generated_reports + export_jobs + documents integration
  - No financial statements engine in P22-A

SECURITY / GOVERNANCE
---------------------
  - Proposed finance.* permissions (separate from hr.payroll.*)
  - Segregation of duties: payroll calculate ≠ finance post
  - Immutable posted journals; signed export downloads
  - Audit retention via finance audit log + document registry
