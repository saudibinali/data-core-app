1) TENANT-FACING READ-ONLY APIs
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Router: artifacts/api-server/src/routes/tenant-subscription.ts

GET /tenant/subscription/summary
  Permission: tenant.subscription.read
  Returns (tenant-safe):
    subscriptionStatus, planName, startDate, endDate, renewalDate,
    gracePeriodEndsAt, accessMode, readOnlyMode, readOnlyReason,
    daysUntilEnd, daysPastEnd, recommendedStatus (advisory label only),
    supportContact (billing contact name/email/phone/role when configured)

GET /tenant/subscription/entitlements
  Permission: tenant.subscription.entitlements.read OR tenant.subscription.read
  Returns modules with enabled status and feature flags (no source/internal IDs)

GET /tenant/subscription/quotas
  Permission: tenant.subscription.quotas.read OR tenant.subscription.read
  Returns quotaKey, label, limitValue, currentUsage, usagePercent, status,
  warningThresholdPercent, unit (no quotaLimitId, source, or audit fields)

Builders: artifacts/api-server/src/lib/tenant-subscription-visibility.ts
  - Reuses P16-A/B/C/E resolvers (subscription, entitlements, quotas, access, policy eval)
  - No mutations, no audit on read, no internalNotes/risk/collection exposure

Config: artifacts/api-server/src/lib/tenant-subscription-config.ts
