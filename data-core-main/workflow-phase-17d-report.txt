1) ACCESS REVIEW SUMMARY RESOLVER
================================================================================
File: artifacts/api-server/src/lib/platform-access-review.ts
Function: buildPlatformAccessReviewSummary()

Returns:
  totalPlatformUsers, activeUsers, disabledUsers, suspendedUsers,
  rootOwners, platformOwners, protectedUsers,
  usersWithCustomOverrides, usersWithCustomGrants, usersWithCustomDenies,
  usersWithCriticalPermissions, usersMissingRecentReview,
  highRiskUsers[], staleUsers[], generatedAt

High-risk criteria (risk level high | critical):
  - isRootOwner → critical
  - effective platform.permissions.update → critical
  - protected platform admin (P17-C rules)
  - effective platform.users.disable / platform.users.role.update
  - custom grant or deny on critical permission catalog
  - stale sensitive login (90+ days or never, with sensitive effective perms)

Stale users: same login threshold applied to users with sensitive effective permissions.
