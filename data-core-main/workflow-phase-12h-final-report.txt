━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Phase 12 delivered a complete, read-only, super-admin-scoped Governance
Console for the Internal Operations Platform. The console spans 8 routes,
14 purpose-built governance components, 15 deterministic visual model maps,
≥16 read-only data hooks, and 5 layered safety contracts.

Every sub-phase remained strictly frontend-only. Zero backend routes were
added. Zero DB migrations were run. Zero mutation controls exist anywhere in
the governance console. All data access is read-only GET, driven by pre-built
Phase 11 API endpoints. All pages are protected by SuperAdminRoute.

The console provides super-admin users with:
  - Live audit chain integrity inspection and forensic event timelines
  - Policy violation review and evidence reference browsing
  - Governance workflow lifecycle tracking
  - Compliance analytics and effectiveness visualization
  - Platform topology and boundary status inspection
  - Cross-layer readiness dimension scoring
  - Evidence package integrity inspection with hash display and section coverage

The design philosophy throughout is: maximum visibility, zero enforcement.
The console surfaces information for human review — it never acts on it.

  - Snapshot timeline visualization
