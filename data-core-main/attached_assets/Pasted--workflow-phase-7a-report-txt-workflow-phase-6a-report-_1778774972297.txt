2 — DESIGN SNAPSHOT SCHEDULER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

صمّم:
GovernanceSnapshotScheduler

داخل:
single-process scheduler model.

المطلوب:
- periodic capture loop
- deterministic interval
- restart-safe behavior
- no overlapping capture cycles

الـ default:
5-minute capture cadence.

صمّم:
كيف scheduler يمر على workspaces.

المطلوب:
- deterministic ordering
- pagination-ready iteration
- skip deleted/inactive workspaces
- bounded batch size

لكن:
لا تبنِ distributed sharding.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

تأكد:
automated capture
يحافظ على:
- immutable snapshots
- append-only inserts
- no history rewriting
- no UPDATE/DELETE لمسارات snapshots

نفّذ:
minimal pruning lifecycle.

الحد الأدنى المطلوب:

- dry-run capable pruning logic

لكن:
