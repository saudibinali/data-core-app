1) RISK COMPUTATION LOGIC
================================================================================

Module: artifacts/api-server/src/lib/commercial-risk-engine.ts

CommercialRiskLevel (computed per tenant, not stored in DB):
  - low
  - medium
  - high
  - critical

Scoring rules (priority order):
  1. No active contract OR expired active contract → critical
  2. ≥2 overdue invoices OR (≥1 overdue + outstanding ≥ 1000 SAR) OR
     (disputed payments + outstanding ≥ 1000) → critical
  3. Any overdue invoice OR outstanding > 0 OR rejected/disputed payments → high
  4. Renewal at_risk / attention_needed readiness OR renewal notice window → medium
  5. Otherwise → low

Signals returned (all computed):
  activeContractExists, daysUntilContractEnd, daysUntilRenewalDate,
  renewalCommitmentStatus, renewalNoticeDays, unpaidInvoiceCount,
  overdueInvoiceCount, outstandingAmount, disputedPaymentCount,
  hasRejectedPayments, hasOverdueInvoices, hasExpiredContract,
  hasMissingBillingContact, hasMissingInvoicePdf, lastPaymentDate,
  lastInvoiceDate, contractEndDate, renewalDate

Data loader (read-only): artifacts/api-server/src/lib/commercial-risk-loader.ts
  Batch-loads workspaces, accounts, contracts, billing contacts, invoices,
  documents, and payment records; feeds computeTenantCommercialRisk().

2) RENEWAL READINESS LOGIC
================================================================================

RenewalReadinessStatus:
  - no_active_contract — no active contract row
  - blocked — active contract expired (end date in past or status expired)
  - at_risk — inside renewal notice window AND commitment not "committed"
  - attention_needed — missing billing contact, missing PDF on issued/shared
    invoice, rejected payments, overdue invoices, or outstanding balance
  - ready — none of the above

Renewal notice window:
  daysUntilRenewalDate or daysUntilContractEnd within renewalNoticeDays
  (from active contract).

Commitment:
  Only "committed" satisfies renewal commitment; pending/other → at_risk
  when notice window is open.

Platform summary (aggregateCommercialRiskSummary):
  Counts by risk level and readiness; total outstanding; overdue invoice count;
  upcoming renewals (renewal within 90 days).

================================================================================

  Permission: commercial.risk.read
  Returns: totalTenants, risk level counts, readiness counts,
           totalOutstandingAmount, overdueInvoiceCount, upcomingRenewalsCount

GET /api/platform/commercial-risk/list
  Permission: commercial.risk.read
  Query filters: riskLevel, renewalReadinessStatus, hasOverdueInvoices,
                 renewalWithinDays

            overdueInvoiceCount, contractEndDate, renewalDate, reasons[]

  Permission: commercial.risk.read
  Returns: full assessment (riskLevel, renewalReadinessStatus, signals,
            reasons[], recommendedActions[])
