2) EFFECTIVE PERMISSION RESOLVER
================================================================================
Library: artifacts/api-server/src/lib/platform-effective-permissions.ts

resolvePlatformUserEffectivePermissions(platformUserId):
  1. Load platform user (workspaceId IS NULL)
  2. rolePermissions from role matrix
  3. Apply grant overrides (add)
  4. Apply deny overrides (remove) — deny wins
  5. root_platform_owner / isRootOwner: full catalog, overrides ignored, restrictedByProtection=true

Returns: rolePermissions[], grantedOverrides[], deniedOverrides[],
         effectivePermissions[], restrictedByProtection

Wiring (documented):
  - requirePlatformPermission / requireAnyPlatformPermission → actor effective set (cached on req)
  - GET /platform/me → effectivePermissions[] from resolver
  - NOT wired: workspace-scoped member RBAC (unchanged)

================================================================================
3) PROTECTION RULES
================================================================================
Library: artifacts/api-server/src/lib/platform-permission-override-policy.ts

  - No self permission modification
  - Non-root cannot modify root/protected user overrides
  - Self-escalation blocked for platform.users.* and platform.permissions.*
  - platform_admin cannot grant permissions they lack (effective set)
  - Root-only grant/deny of sensitive permissions (disable, reactivate, role.update,
    permissions.update, workspaceAccess.update, subscriptions.status.change)
  - Last active platform owner: critical permissions cannot be denied
  - Unknown / tenant / workspace permission codes rejected
  - Reason min 10 characters required
