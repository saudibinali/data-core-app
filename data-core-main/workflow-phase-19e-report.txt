EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P19-E adds PDF report generation (pdf-lib default, optional puppeteer), workspace-
branded HTML templates with ar/en direction, scheduled report schedules with a
DB polling worker, and email delivery of secure download links through the
existing notification queue. All PDFs flow through export_jobs, generated_reports,
signed download tokens, and report_access_logs. No report center UI, Redis, or BI.
