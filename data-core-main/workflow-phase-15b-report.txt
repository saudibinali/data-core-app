1) CONTRACT TERMS MODEL
================================================================================

Table: commercial_contract_terms
Schema: lib/db/src/schema/commercial-contract-terms.ts
Exported via: lib/db/src/schema/index.ts

Fields:
  - id (serial PK)
  - workspaceId (tenant FK → workspaces.id)
  - commercialAccountId (FK → commercial_accounts.id)
  - contractNumber, contractTitle
  - contractStartDate, contractEndDate, renewalDate
  - renewalNoticeDays, contractTermMonths
  - renewalType: manual | auto_renewal | non_renewing | under_negotiation
  - renewalCommitmentStatus: not_started | pending_customer | pending_internal |
    committed | declined | expired
  - contractValue (numeric 14,2), currency
  - billingCycle: monthly | quarterly | semi_annual | annual | custom
  - paymentTerms: due_on_receipt | net_15 | net_30 | net_45 | net_60 | custom
  - internalOwnerUserId (platform super_admin user, workspaceId null)
  - customerDecisionMakerName, customerDecisionMakerEmail
  - renewalNotes
  - status: draft | active | expired | terminated | archived
  - createdBy, updatedBy, createdAt, updatedAt

Rules:
  - Multiple contract terms per tenant allowed.
  - When a contract is set to status=active, other active contracts for the same
    workspace are demoted to archived (one preferred active contract per tenant).
  - No contract file attachments in P15-B.
  - No invoice rows, no payment fields.

DB migration: run `pnpm --filter @workspace/db run push` after Postgres is up.

================================================================================
2) RENEWAL COMMITMENTS MODEL
================================================================================

Renewal commitment is tracked on each contract term via:
  - renewalType — commercial renewal mechanism
  - renewalCommitmentStatus — pipeline state for renewal decision
  - renewalDate, renewalNoticeDays, contractTermMonths
  - customerDecisionMakerName / customerDecisionMakerEmail
  - renewalNotes (free text)
  - internalOwnerUserId (platform owner)

Status changes on the contract (draft/active/expired/terminated/archived) are
separate from renewalCommitmentStatus and require a reason on the dedicated
status endpoint.

6) VALIDATION
================================================================================

  - Tenant must exist; commercial account must exist for tenant
  - commercialAccountId must match tenant account on create
  - Email format for customerDecisionMakerEmail
  - Dates ISO YYYY-MM-DD; contractStartDate <= contractEndDate;
    renewalDate within [start, end] when all present
  - Enum validation for renewalType, renewalCommitmentStatus, billingCycle,
    paymentTerms, status
  - contractValue >= 0; renewalNoticeDays >= 0; contractTermMonths positive int
  - currency whitelist (SAR, USD, EUR, GBP, AED, KWD, BHD, OMR, QAR)
  - internalOwnerUserId must reference platform super_admin (workspaceId null)
  - Status change requires reason (min 10 characters)

11) NEXT PHASE — P15-C: Invoice Records & Uploaded Invoice PDFs
================================================================================

P15-C will add Invoice Records tied to commercial context. Important product rules:

  - The platform will NOT generate accounting invoices.
  - Official invoices are produced by the customer's external accounting system.
  - Finance will UPLOAD the official invoice as a PDF (stored by the platform).
  - Later, tenants will be able to view and download those uploaded invoices from
    the tenant billing portal (portal itself is out of scope until that phase).

P15-B contract terms provide the commercial/renewal context that P15-C invoice
records will reference; no invoice PDF upload exists until P15-C.
