EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
P25-A delivers the first inventory runtime foundation: canonical PostgreSQL tables,
warehouse and item master services, goods receipt persistence linked to procurement
POs, finance readiness signals, inventory policies, permissions, document registry
integration for GRN attachments, five report definitions, monitoring APIs, and
smoke tests. Receipt post updates PO received quantities but does NOT mutate stock
balances or create movement ledger rows (deferred to P25-B).

No movement posting engine, barcode scanning, MRP, AP, accounting posting, or
destructive migrations were introduced. P25-B was NOT started.
