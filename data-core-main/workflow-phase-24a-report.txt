WORKFLOW SUMMARY
----------------
Defined canonical procurement lifecycles:
- Purchase Request: draft → submitted → approved/rejected → converted → closed
- RFQ: draft → sent → closed → awarded
- Purchase Order: draft → submitted → approved/rejected → sent → acknowledged → closed

Approvals:
- Use Workflow Engine approval capabilities and normalize linkage via
  procurement_approval_links to avoid ticket-only coupling.

Corrections:
- Non-destructive revision strategy; avoid mutating approved/sent artifacts in-place.
